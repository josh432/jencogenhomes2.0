Why work with me?


  • It's not a job, but a family tradition.

    To me, real estate is not a job, but a family tradition. Both my parents and grandfather built as well as sold homes, a passion which has become ingrained in me. A hands-on kind of person, I even have my own compound miter saw!

  • Wisdom of a local

    As the mother of two school age children, I understand the pressures facing families in this area. Selling or buying a home can be a daunting undertaking. When you work with me, I will empower you with information and constantly be available for you, so that when you make decisions you feel fully confident.

  • Negotiate for your interests

    Every transaction will inevitably have some bumps, and I will figure out solutions to navigate them. It is my priority to work both sides of the transaction to negotiate your interests, and often, both of you can “win.”

  • Giving back to the community

    Living in the Bay Area is a privilege, and giving back to our community is something I am deeply committed to. Over the years, I’ve volunteered at an animal shelter and four schools. I am the President of the Parents’ Association as well as a Trustee on the Board at a Moraga school.

  • Always at your service

    With an M.B.A. in International Management, I enjoy meeting people from all over. I’ve even lived in/traveled to over 26 countries. My excellent professional service extended to corporate and retail customers as Customer Relationship Manager at Cisco Systems—for their largest customer, and retail management. For fun, I enjoy spending time with my family, wine tasting, cooking and baking, hiking, skiing, and hanging out with people who make me laugh.
    I look forward to finding out which doors I can open for you!
